Dear Harshini,

When everything was moving fast, I felt like I was in a state of grace because you were there, keeping me calm when I wanted to panic. You never looked at my dreams and called them huge or unrealistic; instead, you made me feel like we could be the kings and queens of anything we wanted.

I used to think my purpose was just to work hard, long story short. But you showed me that it's nice to have a friend, a partner, a lover to actually live life with. You taught me that the best things aren't things.

I'm so excited to be on this journey with you. Long live all the magic we made and are going to make. Thank you for being the best thing that's ever been mine.

Love,
Gopi
